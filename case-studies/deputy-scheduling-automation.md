## Overview

Implemented scheduling automations and alerts within Deputy to reduce manual effort and compliance risk.

## Business problem

- Manual rostering created errors and compliance risk.
- Data inconsistencies between Deputy and payroll caused rework.
- Leaders needed visibility of labour costs and coverage.

## Approach

- Designed rostering rules, alerts, and approvals aligned to award requirements.
- Integrated Deputy with payroll and reporting systems for accurate data flow.
- Trained managers on exception handling and audit processes.

## Outcome

- Reduced manual effort and improved compliance confidence.
- Cleaner payroll inputs with fewer adjustments required.
- Clear visibility of scheduling, labour costs, and coverage for leaders.

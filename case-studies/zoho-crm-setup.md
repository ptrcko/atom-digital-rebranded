## Overview

Delivered a structured Zoho CRM rollout that aligned with sales and service processes.

## Business problem

- Sales and service teams lacked a unified system and consistent data.
- Manual processes slowed handoffs and reporting.
- Adoption risked stalling without clear training and governance.

## Approach

- Mapped customer journeys into CRM pipelines, fields, and automation.
- Migrated and cleansed data, establishing standards for ongoing quality.
- Produced training and playbooks to embed consistent usage.

## Outcome

- Unified customer data and workflows across teams.
- Faster handoffs and improved reporting on pipeline and service performance.
- Confident adoption supported by training and governance.

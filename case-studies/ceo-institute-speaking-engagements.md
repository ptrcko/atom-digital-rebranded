## Overview

Provided keynote sessions and workshops for the CEO Institute, sharing practical approaches to technology governance, AI adoptio
n, and operational risk management tailored to executive audiences.

## Business problem

- Executives sought pragmatic guidance on technology and AI without vendor bias.
- Boards needed clarity on risk, governance, and measurable outcomes.
- Leaders wanted language and frameworks they could use with their teams immediately.

## Approach

- Curated sessions with industry examples, checklists, and practical next steps.
- Facilitated discussions to align technology ambitions with risk appetite.
- Provided follow-up materials that leaders could adapt for their organisations.

## Outcome

- Executives left with clear actions for governance, AI pilots, and operational uplift.
- Boards received concise talking points and measures to track progress.
- Strengthened confidence to engage with technology stakeholders and vendors.

## Overview

Resolved deliverability issues through domain alignment, authentication, and content governance.

## Business problem

- Marketing and operational emails were landing in spam or failing authentication checks.
- Sender reputation was deteriorating, impacting critical communications.
- Teams needed sustainable governance to avoid future issues.

## Approach

- Implemented SPF, DKIM, DMARC, and alignment across sending domains and platforms.
- Audited content and sending practices, introducing controls and segmentation.
- Set up monitoring, reporting, and runbooks to maintain reputation.

## Outcome

- Improved deliverability and restored sender reputation.
- Clear processes for future campaigns and operational communications.
- Leadership visibility into deliverability metrics and risk indicators.

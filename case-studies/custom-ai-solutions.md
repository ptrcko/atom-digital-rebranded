## Overview

Delivered custom AI solutions covering data preparation, model selection, and deployment.

## Business problem

- Teams wanted AI-enabled processes but lacked clear governance and delivery structure.
- Data readiness and stakeholder alignment were inconsistent across departments.
- Leadership needed assurance on safety, compliance, and ROI.

## Approach

- Scoped opportunities with clear hypotheses and success metrics.
- Prepared data pipelines, selected models, and implemented responsible use controls.
- Ran pilots with training and feedback loops before scaling.

## Outcome

- AI capabilities delivered with documented controls and monitoring.
- Stakeholders gained confidence through measurable pilot results.
- Playbooks provided for future AI initiatives across the organisation.

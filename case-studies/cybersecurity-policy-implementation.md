## Overview

Created and implemented cybersecurity policies tailored to organisational risk.

## Business problem

- Policies were outdated, inconsistent, or lacked enforcement.
- Teams needed clear guidance on roles, responsibilities, and incident handling.
- Vendor controls and onboarding requirements were not standardised.

## Approach

- Developed policy suite aligned to risk appetite and regulatory expectations.
- Introduced training, simulations, and communication plans for staff and vendors.
- Implemented incident response workflows and playbooks with escalation paths.

## Outcome

- Up-to-date policies embedded in day-to-day operations.
- Improved awareness and response readiness across teams and suppliers.
- Clear vendor requirements reducing third-party security risk.

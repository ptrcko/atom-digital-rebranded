## Overview

Delivered a combined CTO/COO review covering technology stack, vendor performance, and operating model.

## Business problem

- Leadership needed clarity on technology risks, vendor performance, and operational gaps.
- Service quality and governance required improvement without disrupting customers.
- Stakeholders wanted a practical roadmap that balanced quick wins and longer-term change.

## Approach

- Assessed architecture, integrations, and vendor contracts alongside operational processes.
- Prioritised risks and opportunities, aligning them to measurable business outcomes.
- Presented a staged roadmap with ownership, investment needs, and success metrics.

## Outcome

- Clear view of current risks and the steps to remediate them.
- Prioritised initiatives with agreed owners and timelines.
- Leadership confidence through concise reporting and governance recommendations.

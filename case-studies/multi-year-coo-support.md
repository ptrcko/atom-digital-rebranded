## Overview

Partnered over multiple years to establish operating rhythms, performance reporting, and cross-functional coordination.

## Business problem

- Rapid growth required stronger governance and delivery discipline.
- Teams lacked consistent KPIs, reporting, and escalation paths.
- Leadership needed confidence that execution matched strategy.

## Approach

- Designed and embedded operating cadence across leadership and delivery teams.
- Established performance reporting, risk registers, and decision logs.
- Facilitated cross-functional alignment and coaching for emerging leaders.

## Outcome

- Stable growth with clear accountability and predictable delivery.
- Executives received concise, consistent reporting to inform decisions.
- Teams adopted sustainable rhythms that persisted beyond the engagement.

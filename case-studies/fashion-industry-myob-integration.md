## Overview

Connected MYOB with sales and fulfilment channels to improve data accuracy and financial reporting.

## Business problem

- Data gaps between ecommerce, logistics, and finance systems caused reconciliation issues.
- Seasonal peaks amplified errors and manual workload.
- Leadership needed reliable reporting across inventory and financials.

## Approach

- Mapped data flows between MYOB, ecommerce, and logistics platforms with clear ownership.
- Implemented integrations and reconciliation routines to maintain accuracy.
- Established support processes for peak periods and change control.

## Outcome

- More accurate financial and inventory reporting with fewer manual adjustments.
- Smoother peak trading periods with clearer incident and escalation paths.
- Leadership visibility into performance through timely, reliable data.

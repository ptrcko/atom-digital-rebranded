## Overview

Mapped guest journeys, rostering, and inventory processes to remove bottlenecks and improve consistency across venues.

## Business problem

- Service quality and profitability were inconsistent across venues, affecting guest experience and margins.
- Rostering and inventory processes caused delays, waste, and stock inaccuracies.
- Leadership lacked real-time visibility into operational performance to intervene early.

## Approach

- Documented guest journeys and backstage processes to identify constraints impacting speed and service quality.
- Redesigned rostering, ordering, and inventory controls with clear ownership and escalation paths.
- Implemented reporting linking service metrics to financial outcomes, giving leaders actionable insights.
- Trained venue managers and team leads to maintain processes and coach staff.

## Outcome

- Improved service consistency and guest satisfaction across venues.
- Better cost control through disciplined rostering and inventory management.
- Leadership visibility enabling faster interventions and coaching through aligned reporting.

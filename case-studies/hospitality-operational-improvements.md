## Overview

Mapped guest journeys, rostering, and inventory processes to remove bottlenecks.

## Business problem

- Service quality and profitability were inconsistent across venues.
- Rostering and inventory processes caused delays and waste.
- Leadership lacked real-time visibility into operational performance.

## Approach

- Documented guest journeys and backstage processes to identify constraints.
- Redesigned rostering, ordering, and inventory controls with clear ownership.
- Implemented reporting linking service metrics to financial outcomes.

## Outcome

- Improved service consistency and guest satisfaction.
- Better cost control through disciplined rostering and inventory management.
- Leadership visibility enabling faster interventions and coaching.

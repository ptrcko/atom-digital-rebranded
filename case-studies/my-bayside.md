## Overview

Provided fractional CTO/COO leadership to stabilise systems, improve data visibility, and guide vendor delivery.

## Business problem

- Fragmented systems and inconsistent reporting were slowing customer delivery.
- Vendors were not aligned to commercial priorities or service expectations.
- Teams needed clearer roles, rituals, and escalation paths.

## Approach

- Rapid assessment of architecture, data flows, and vendor contracts.
- Introduced delivery cadence with stand-ups, risk reviews, and executive updates.
- Simplified reporting to give leadership visibility of commitments and risks.

## Outcome

- More predictable delivery and improved customer communication.
- Data visibility that supported quicker decision-making across teams.
- Vendors operating to clearer governance and performance measures.

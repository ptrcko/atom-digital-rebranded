Understand how fractional CTO or COO support can be structured for your organisation. Engagements can be scoped by outcomes, hours, or project phases, ensuring you invest at the right level.

What influences cost:
- Complexity of technology stack and operational footprint
- Required governance, reporting, and stakeholder management
- Urgency of delivery and level of change management needed

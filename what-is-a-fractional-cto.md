title: "What is a Fractional CTO?"
layout: page
description: "Understand how a fractional CTO brings strategic technology leadership without the full-time overhead."
permalink: "/what-is-a-fractional-cto/"
---

A fractional CTO delivers senior technology leadership on a flexible basis. You gain strategic direction, architecture governance, and delivery oversight without committing to a full-time executive. The model suits organisations that need expert guidance to scale or remediate projects while managing cost.

Key benefits include:
- Objective decision-making on platforms, data, and security
- Hands-on guidance for teams and vendors to ship reliably
- Confidence that technology choices align with commercial outcomes
